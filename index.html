About Our School

Nurturing Leaders, Learners, and Creators

Founded in 2015, first as a Nursery School, Pamville Schools Nsangi has now grown into two main establishments which includes Pamville Junior School (PJS) and Pamville International Pre-School Uganda (PIPU).

The two institutions aim to provide warm, supportive, and academically stimulating environments in which all learners are happy, safe, secure and are eager to learn and capable of discovering their individual gifts and nurturing their dreams. At Pamville Schools Nsangi, every child matters!

Pamville Schools Nsangi provides a well-rounded educational experience with quality academic programs and a wide range of co-curricular activities that stimulate the learners' abilities to innovate and critically think.

To provide a rigorous, holistic education that inspires creativity, sparks intellectual curiosity, and equips students with the values and skills needed to lead with integrity and make a positive impact in the world.

To be a global benchmark for educational innovation, recognized for cultivating resilient, creative, and compassionate citizens who successfully navigate and shape a dynamic global landscape.

We believe that education must extend beyond academic transcripts to form deep character, strength of spirit, and dynamic collaboration.
